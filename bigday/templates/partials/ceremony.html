Location: Jordan Pines Campground

Getting There and Away

Jordan Pines Campground is located about 30 minutes from the mouth of Big Cottonwood Canyon, along the beginning of the Donut Falls hiking trail.

There is a large parking lot along Big Cottonwood Canyon road that we recommend you park in.

From there, simply walk along the large trail/small road on the south side (right side when driving up-canyon). Jordan Pines Campground is about 1/4 mile up the trail. We will also have a golf cart shuttle if you prefer not to walk to the campground.
